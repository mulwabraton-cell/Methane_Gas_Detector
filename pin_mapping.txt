MQ-4 Analog: D34
==========================================================
REVISED HARDWARE MAPPING: RAW MQ-4 SENSOR (WINSEN)

PIN CONFIGURATION:
- Heater (H): Top-Left & Bottom-Right -> 5V / GND
- Anode (A): Top-Right & Mid-Right -> 5V
- Cathode (B): Mid-Left & Bot-Left -> Signal Out (to Divider)

OUTPUT PATH:
Sensor (B) -> 1k Ohm -> GPIO 34 (Analog) -> 2k Ohm -> GND
